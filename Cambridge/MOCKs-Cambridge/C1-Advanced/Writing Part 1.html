Writing Test · 2 Tasks
90 minutes · Both parts compulsory
You must complete both parts. In Part 1 the question is compulsory. In Part 2 you must choose one of three tasks. Write your answers in 220–260 words in an appropriate style.

Part 1 · Essay (compulsory)

You must answer this question. Write your answer in 220–260 words in an appropriate style.

Your class has listened to a podcast about factors that contribute to people\u2019s understanding of history. You have made the notes below:

Factors that contribute to people\u2019s understanding of history:

  • school lessons
  • public monuments
  • museums

Some opinions expressed in the podcast:

  • "Some teachers make learning history really interesting."
  • "Statues in public areas show what local people think is important."
  • "You can visit many museums online."

Write an essay for your tutor discussing two of the factors in your notes. You should identify which factor you think contributes more to people\u2019s understanding of history, and provide reasons to justify your choice.

You may, if you wish, make use of the opinions expressed in the podcast, but you should use your own words as far as possible.
