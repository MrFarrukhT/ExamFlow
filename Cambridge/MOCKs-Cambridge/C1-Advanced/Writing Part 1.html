Writing Test · 2 Tasks
90 minutes · Both tasks compulsory
Part 1 — Essay (compulsory): Write your answer in 220–260 words.
Part 2 — Choose ONE task: Write your answer in 220–260 words.

Part 1: Essay

Task: Your class has watched a panel discussion about how schools can prepare students for the workplace. You have made the notes below.

Some opinions expressed in the discussion:

  • "Practical skills should be taught alongside academic subjects."
  • "Schools should teach communication and teamwork."
  • "Students need real-world work experience before they leave school."

Write an essay discussing two of the points in your notes. You should explain which preparation you think is most important, giving reasons to support your opinion.

Part 2: Task 2

Task: A travel magazine has invited readers to send in reviews of a museum or art gallery they have visited recently. Your review should describe the museum or gallery, explain what you found most interesting about it, and say whether you would recommend it to other visitors.

Write your review.
